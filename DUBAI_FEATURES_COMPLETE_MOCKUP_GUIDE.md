## üéØ Golden Path User Flow (As Mockup)

### Entry Point: Market Heatmap
User opens app ‚Üí Sees Dubai map with color-coded ROI zones ‚Üí Clicks on "Business Bay" (glowing green)

### Step 1: Project Selection
Grid of high-ROI projects appears ‚Üí User clicks "Emaar Beachfront Vista" ‚Üí Header shows 4K drone flyover mockup

### Step 2: Visual Validation
- Opens **Virtual Staging** tool ‚Üí Sees Modern furnished 2BR
- Toggles to **Golden Hour View** ‚Üí Sees Burj Khalifa skyline lit up

### Step 3: Data Verification
- Opens **Market Transparency** tab
- Sees **DLD Data**: Prices up 12.4% in 6 months
- Reviews **Construction Tracker**: 68% complete, on schedule

### Step 4: ROI Analysis
- Opens **ROI Calculator**
- Compares Short-term (11.2% yield) vs Long-term (7.8% yield)
- Decides on Airbnb strategy

### Step 5: Proximity Check
- Opens **Custom Proximity Engine**
- Adds "DIFC Office" and "GEMS School"
- Sees 12 min and 9 min drive times (perfect!)

### Step 6: Comparison
- Adds to **Comparison Tray**
- Adds competitor project
- Realizes Driven Properties has better payment plan (60/40 vs 80/20)

### Step 7: Silent Interest
- Clicks **"Save to My Portfolio"**
- App asks: "Would you like a private briefing?"
- Selects: **"Send PDF to email only‚ÄîNo calls"**
- Documents downloaded to **No-Spam Vault**

### Step 8: Timeline Review
- Reviews **Investment Roadmap**
- Sees exact 10% installment schedule over 3 years
- Understands DLD fees and Oqood registration

### Exit:
Information saved. No agents call. Two days later: notification about higher-floor unit available.
